Our Story

"We are dedicated to providing you with easy access to healthcare. Our doctors and medical team prioritize patient care and are well supported by warm and caring clinic staff."

Founded in 2021, Polyclinic Saint Robert in Kigali Town is a medical facility providing an entire spectrum of medical services through better healthcare services and treatment using advanced equipment and skills in Rwanda with the aim to change the future of patient care and treatment.

Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, compassionate healthcare services to our community, ensuring the well-being and safety of our patients at all times.

Vision

Our vision is to become a leading healthcare provider in Rwanda, known for excellence in patient care, advanced medical technology, and a commitment to community health.

FAQ

Why Should I Choose Polyclinic Saint Robert?

We are committed to providing high-quality healthcare services and ensuring the safety and well-being of our patients. Our team is dedicated to improving the quality of patient care and health services within the community.

What Are Your Visiting Hours?

Our clinic is open 24/7 to provide you with the best medical care. You can visit us anytime for emergencies or scheduled appointments.

How Many Visitors Are Allowed?

To ensure the safety and comfort of our patients, we allow one visitor per patient at a time. Exceptions may be made for special circumstances.
